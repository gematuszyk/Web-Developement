Use Batteries and Steel Wool
  1. First make a tinder nest out of any dry plant material that easily catches fire. You can use dry grass, leaves, small sticks or bark.
  2. Find a battery and locate the battery terminals, the two circular prongs on the top of battery. Any battery will work but 9-volt batteries will ignite the quickest.
  3. Take your steel wool and rub it on the battery terminals, the finer the steel wool the better.
  4. Continue to create fiction by rubbing the steel wool on the batteries and then blow gently on the steel wool as it starts to glow.
  5. Once the steel wool is flowing brightly, transfer the steel wool to your tinder nest quickly continuing to blow lightly.
  6. Add increasingly larger pieces of dry wood to build your fire once the tinder nest has ignited.
